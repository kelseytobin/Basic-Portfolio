About Me

I am a perceptive, compassionate, and curious gal in my late 20's looking to make my mark on the world. I spend a great deal of my time thinking about how I could be challenging myself and contributing to the world in a larger way. I like to think of myself as awkwardly charming, with a heavy inclination towards being a goober.


Living in a mountain town community and having the experiences I have had in the outdoor environment here, has shaped my perspective in a way that will ultimately last. I find strength and renewal in open space yet I am not immune to the limitations of living in the middle of nowhere Wyoming in a tourism based economy. I hope through this class and the journey it will take me on after it ends, I can find stimulation and challenge outside of my everyday.
